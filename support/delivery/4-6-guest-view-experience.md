# What does my client see when they open the link?

When your client opens the delivery email and clicks through, they land directly on the gallery — no password or app download required for that first view. Behind the scenes, their browser is using the link to decrypt the gallery on their own device.

## What they can do

- Browse the full gallery
- Favourite photos
- Leave comments or flag favourites, if you've enabled that interaction
- Download photos, if you've turned that on for this delivery (see [Controlling what a client can download](./4-4-client-download-controls.md))
- Generate their own share link, only if you've explicitly enabled re-sharing (see [Can my client re-share the gallery?](./4-5-client-share-permission.md))

## What they can't do

Upload, edit captions, delete anything, or invite other people. The client role is intentionally view-and-respond only.

## If your client wants a P3 account of their own

After viewing, clients are typically prompted with an option to save the gallery to a full P3 account of their own, if they'd like one — useful if they want to keep accessing it long-term, organize it alongside other galleries, or eventually create their own. This is entirely optional from their side.

## What you'll see on your end

You'll be able to see engagement for this gallery — which photos your client viewed, favourited, or downloaded — from your client's profile. This is part of how the delivery feature works, so it's worth letting clients know if that's relevant to your working relationship.
