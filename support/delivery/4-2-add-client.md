# Adding a client to your account

From **Clients → Add Client**, fill in:

- **Client name** — required
- **Client email** — required
- **Job notes** — optional, and private. This field is labelled clearly in the app as visible to you only — your client never sees it, so it's a good place for shoot details, special requests, or delivery deadlines.

Click **Create client** to save the profile.

## What happens next

Creating a client profile doesn't send them anything yet — it's just a record on your side. P3 checks in the background whether that email belongs to an existing P3 account; if it does, you'll be able to assign a gallery to them right away. If not, the profile is simply marked as not yet set up, and things proceed normally once you assign a gallery and send an invite.

## If the email is already in use

If you already have a client profile with that email, you'll see an error telling you so — each client email needs to be unique within your own client list (though two different photographers can absolutely have clients with the same email; profiles are fully isolated per photographer).

## Next step

Once your client profile exists, assign a gallery to them — see [Sending a delivery link to a client](./4-3-send-delivery-link.md).
