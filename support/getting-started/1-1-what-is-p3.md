# What is P3 and who is it for?

P3 is a private photo delivery and sharing platform built end-to-end encrypted from the ground up. Your photos are encrypted on your device before they ever leave it, so P3's own servers never see the actual image content — only the encrypted bytes.

It's built for two kinds of people:

- **Photographers** who need a professional way to deliver finished galleries to clients without relying on tools that weren't built for that job.
- **Personal users** who want a private, ad-free way to share photos with people who matter, without handing their memories to a platform that scans, mines, or monetizes them.

## What makes P3 different

Tools like Dropbox, Google Photos, or WeTransfer can technically see and access your files — their systems are built to read your content. P3 cannot. This isn't a marketing claim, it's an architectural fact: photos are encrypted on your device before upload, and P3 never holds the key needed to decrypt them.
