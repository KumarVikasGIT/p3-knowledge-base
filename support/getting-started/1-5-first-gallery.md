# Creating your first gallery and uploading photos

Once key setup is done, you'll land on your dashboard. Here's how to create your first gallery.

## Steps

1. Click **New Gallery** from the dashboard or galleries list.
2. Name your gallery and set basic details.
3. Upload your photos — drag and drop, or select files from your device.
4. Once uploads finish, invite collaborators or generate a share link.

## What to expect during upload

Uploads happen client-side first — your browser encrypts each photo before it leaves your device. This means larger batches or slower connections will take a bit longer than a typical cloud upload. That's the trade-off for the encryption happening on your end rather than P3's servers.

## If something goes wrong mid-upload

If part of a batch fails partway through, see [Some photos uploaded but others didn't](../troubleshooting/8-2-upload-partial-failure.md) for what to do — you won't need to start over or risk duplicate uploads.
