# Creating and sharing a gallery link

From an open gallery, open the **Share** panel (Owner only).

If the gallery hasn't been shared before, you'll see a **Generate link** option. This does two things: turns the gallery's visibility to link-only, and creates the share link itself. Once generated, the link is shown and ready to copy and send.

## What's in the link

The link contains a special part that lets a viewer's browser unlock the gallery's content directly on their device, without needing a P3 account or password. This is what makes link-only sharing work without friction — but it's also why links should be treated carefully. See [Is a share link secure?](../encryption/2-4-share-link-security.md) for the full explanation.

## One link per gallery

P3 gives each gallery a single active share link at a time, rather than separate customizable links per recipient. If you need to send a gallery to several people via link, you'll send the same link to all of them. If you need to control access more precisely per person — different download permissions, the ability to revoke just one person's access — invite them individually as collaborators instead, using [Gallery access levels: Viewer, Editor, Owner](./3-4-invite-access-levels.md).

## After sharing

You can turn off sharing entirely, or generate a new link to replace the old one — see [Revoking or regenerating a share link](./3-6-share-link-revoke.md) for what each option actually does, including an important nuance about what regenerating does and doesn't undo.
