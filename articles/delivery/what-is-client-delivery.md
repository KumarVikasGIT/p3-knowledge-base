# What is the client delivery feature?

Client delivery is P3's photographer-to-client workflow — a purpose-built way to send finished galleries to clients, distinct from inviting someone as a regular collaborator.

It's available on Professional accounts (see [Personal vs Professional](../getting-started/1-3-account-types.md)), and the full client management interface requires the Pro plan — see [What are the P3 plans and what's included?](../billing/6-1-plan-overview.md) for the exact breakdown.

## How it's different from inviting a collaborator

Collaborators are people working *with* you on a gallery — they can often upload, caption, and manage content. Clients are people you're *delivering finished work to* — they view, favourite, comment, and download (if you allow it), but never upload or edit. Client delivery also doesn't require your client to have a P3 account before you start — you can set everything up, and access activates automatically once they register.

## What's included

- A dedicated client list, separate from your gallery collaborators
- Branded delivery emails when you're ready to share
- No-login gallery viewing for your client, once they're set up
- Per-gallery controls over downloading and re-sharing
- Visibility into client engagement — which photos were viewed, favourited, or downloaded, so you know what resonated

Worth knowing: that engagement visibility is part of how the feature works, not a separate setting — if you're delivering to clients, you'll be able to see which photos they interacted with.
