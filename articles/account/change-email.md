# Can I change my account email?

Not yet — your email address is read-only in the current version of P3. You can update your display name and account type freely from Settings, but the email tied to your account at registration can't be changed through the app right now.

## Why this is the case for now

Changing an email safely requires re-verifying the new address before it takes effect, which depends on parts of P3's email system that aren't built out yet. Rather than offer a change that doesn't fully verify the new address, P3 doesn't expose the option until that's ready.

## If you need your email updated

Contact support — see [How to contact P3 support](../troubleshooting/contact-support.md). Include your current account email and the reason for the change so it can be handled manually in the meantime.
