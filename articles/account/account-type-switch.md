# Switching between Personal and Professional

From **Settings → Account**, select the account type card you want — Personal or Professional — and confirm the change. See [Personal vs Professional — which should I choose?](../getting-started/account-types.md) for what each one unlocks.

## What happens when you switch

Switching to **Professional** unlocks the client delivery features described in the [Client Delivery](../delivery/what-is-client-delivery.md) category. Your existing galleries and collaborators aren't affected — you're simply gaining access to additional tools.

Switching back to **Personal** asks you to confirm, and if you have active client deliveries set up, you'll see an additional warning about that before proceeding. Switching account types doesn't delete your client profiles or delivered galleries — those records and the access already granted stay intact even if the client-facing tools become hidden — but it does mean you'll lose the dedicated client management interface unless you switch back to Professional.

## What it doesn't affect

Account type is separate from your plan. Switching doesn't change your storage limit, gallery limit, or billing — see [Personal vs Professional](../getting-started/account-types.md) for the full explanation of that distinction.
