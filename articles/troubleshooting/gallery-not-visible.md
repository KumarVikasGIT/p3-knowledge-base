# A gallery I was invited to isn't showing up

A few different things can cause this — work through them in order.

## The invite is still pending

The most common cause: granting you access requires the inviting photographer or collaborator to be online at some point after you accept, since P3's encryption model means the access key has to be handed over directly between active devices, not relayed by the server while one side is offline. If they haven't been active in P3 since you accepted, your invite is technically complete on your end but still waiting on theirs. See [Inviting someone to a gallery](../getting-started/1-6-invite-collaborator.md) for the full explanation of why this works this way.

This usually resolves on its own once the other person opens P3 again — there's nothing broken, just a timing gap.

## The invite expired

Invites are valid for a limited time. If too long passed between when you were invited and when you tried to accept, the invite link may have expired — ask the gallery owner to send a fresh invite.

## You're logged into the wrong account

If you have more than one email you use, double check the invite went to the account you're currently logged into. Invites are tied to a specific email address.

## What to check

- Confirm with the person who invited you that they've opened P3 recently
- Check the invite email for an expiration note, and ask for a resend if it's been a while
- Confirm you're logged into the correct account

## If it's still not appearing after that

Contact support — see [How to contact P3 support](./8-12-contact-support.md) — with the gallery name and roughly when you were invited.
