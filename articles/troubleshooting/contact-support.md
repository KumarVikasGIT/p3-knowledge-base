# How to contact P3 support

If something in this knowledge base hasn't resolved your issue, reach out directly and a member of the team will help.

## What to include

The more context you can give upfront, the faster support can help — especially since P3's encryption means support genuinely can't see your photo content to diagnose things by looking at it directly. Useful details:

- Your account email
- A screenshot, if the issue is visual (an error message, a broken state, anything on-screen)
- Steps to reproduce the issue, if you can — what you did right before it happened
- The gallery name, if the issue is specific to one gallery

## Response time

Support aims to respond within 2 business days.

## For billing-specific questions

See [How to request a refund](../billing/6-6-request-refund.md) for refund-specific contact details, or reach billing@p3.app directly for other billing matters.

## For a security concern

If you've found or suspect a security vulnerability, please don't post details publicly — reach out through a private channel so it can be addressed responsibly before any public disclosure. This protects both you and other P3 users while a fix is developed.

## For anything else

Use the general support contact form or email, available from the Support section of the app.
