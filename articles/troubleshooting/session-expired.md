# I was logged out unexpectedly

Your photos are safe — being logged out only affects your current session, not your account or any encrypted content.

## Why this happens

P3 keeps you logged in using a session that refreshes itself automatically in the background, well before it would actually expire — so under normal use, you shouldn't notice this happening at all. You'll typically only get logged out unexpectedly if:

- You've been away from P3 for an extended period and your session genuinely expired in the meantime
- You logged out from another tab or device — logging out in P3 ends the session everywhere it was open, not just where you clicked it
- Your browser cleared its stored session data, which can happen with certain privacy settings or browser cleanup tools

## What to do

Just log back in. If your device already has your encryption key stored locally, you'll go straight back to your galleries with no further steps needed.

## If it happens repeatedly, especially soon after logging in

This is unusual and worth a closer look — check whether your browser is configured to clear site data automatically (some privacy-focused browser settings do this aggressively), since that would explain frequent unexpected logouts. If that's not it, contact support — see [How to contact P3 support](./contact-support.md).
