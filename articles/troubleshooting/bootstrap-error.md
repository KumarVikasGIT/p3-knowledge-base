# "Something went wrong loading your session"

This message appears when P3 can't complete its normal startup sequence — the checks it runs every time you open the app to confirm your session, account state, and encryption key are all in order.

## What's usually behind it

This is most often a temporary network blip during that startup sequence, rather than a sign of a deeper problem with your account or data — none of your photos or galleries are affected by this error, since it's about loading the app itself, not your content.

## What to do

The screen will show two options:

- **Try again** — reloads the page and re-runs the startup sequence. This resolves the large majority of cases, especially if the original cause was a brief connectivity issue.
- **Log out** — clears your session entirely and returns you to the login screen, useful if "Try again" doesn't help and you suspect something about your stored session specifically is the problem.

Logging out and back in is safe — it doesn't affect your galleries or require you to re-enter your recovery phrase, as long as your device already has your key stored locally.

## If it keeps happening

If this error appears repeatedly across multiple attempts, it's worth noting the small technical detail shown on the error screen (it's there specifically to help diagnose cases like this) and reaching out to support with it — see [How to contact P3 support](./contact-support.md).
