# What is P3's refund policy?

This covers all paid P3 plans. Since the Free plan isn't charged, there's nothing to refund on it.

## 30-day satisfaction guarantee

If you're not satisfied with a paid plan for any reason, contact P3 within **30 days of your first charge** on that plan and you'll get a full refund — no questions asked. This applies to your first subscription payment on a given plan.

## Renewal charges

If you're charged a renewal you didn't intend — for example, you meant to cancel before it billed again — contact P3 within **7 days of the renewal charge**. The renewal will be refunded and the subscription cancelled. After 7 days, renewal charges are non-refundable.

## Partial-period cancellations

P3 doesn't prorate refunds for cancelling partway through a billing period. When you cancel, your plan stays active through the end of the period you've already paid for, then moves to Free — see [How to downgrade or cancel your subscription](./6-3-downgrade-plan.md).

## Annual plans

Annual subscribers who cancel within 30 days get a full refund, same as monthly. After 30 days, P3 may refund the remaining unused months minus a 10% administrative fee, at its discretion — reach out and this can be discussed directly.

## What isn't refundable

- Accounts suspended for violating P3's Terms of Service
- Charges older than 30 days (outside the annual-plan provision above)
- Partial months on a cancelled monthly plan
