# How do I get an invoice or receipt?

From **Settings → Plan → Billing → Receipts & invoices**, you'll find a record of your past payments. Select any payment to download a P3-branded PDF invoice with the payment date, amount, plan, billing period, and transaction ID.

## If you need the GST details

P3 is operated by a GST-registered sole proprietorship in India. Invoices include the relevant GST information needed for business expense reporting where applicable.

## If you can't find what you need in-app

You can also access your full payment history directly through the Razorpay billing portal, linked from the same Billing section — this gives you Razorpay's own transaction records as an alternative source if you ever need to cross-reference.
