# How to downgrade or cancel your subscription

From **Settings → Plan**, select **Cancel subscription**. You'll see exactly what changes before you confirm — cancellation doesn't take effect immediately.

## What happens when you cancel

Your current plan stays fully active until the end of your current billing period — you're not cut off the moment you cancel, and there's no partial refund for the unused remainder of the period (see [What is P3's refund policy?](./6-5-refund-policy.md) for the exceptions to this). Once the period ends, your account moves to the Free plan automatically.

## If you're over the Free plan's limits when that happens

If your storage or gallery count exceeds Free plan limits once your paid plan ends, P3 doesn't delete anything immediately. Galleries above what Free allows become read-only rather than being removed, and you'll have a meaningful window to either reduce usage or upgrade again before anything is at risk — see [What happens if I exceed my plan limits?](./6-8-plan-limits-enforcement.md) for the full timeline. The intent is to give you a real runway to react, not to surprise you with sudden data loss the moment a subscription lapses.

## Changing your mind

If you cancel and then decide you want to keep your plan after all, you can typically reactivate before the current billing period ends without needing to resubscribe from scratch. After the period has ended and you've moved to Free, you'd start a new subscription instead — see [How to upgrade your plan](./6-2-upgrade-plan.md).
