# How to request a refund

From **Settings → Plan → Billing**, select **Request a refund** and choose the payment you're asking about. You'll pick a reason and confirm — P3 checks eligibility automatically based on the timing rules in [What is P3's refund policy?](./6-5-refund-policy.md), so you'll know right away if the payment qualifies.

## What to include

- Your account email
- The charge date and amount
- A brief note on what went wrong — optional, but it helps if there's something P3 could actually fix

## If you'd rather not use the in-app flow

You can also email billing@p3.app directly with the same details above. This works just as well and is handled by the same team — useful if you're having trouble with the in-app option for any reason, or simply prefer email.

## What happens next

You'll see a confirmation once your request is submitted, along with an estimated processing time. Approved refunds typically take 5–10 business days to process, though how quickly it shows up in your account afterward depends on your bank or card issuer — that part is outside P3's control once the refund has been initiated.

## If your payment isn't eligible

If the payment falls outside the refund windows described in [What is P3's refund policy?](./6-5-refund-policy.md), you'll see why directly rather than a generic rejection. If you think there's a special circumstance worth considering, email billing@p3.app and explain — exceptions are evaluated case by case.
