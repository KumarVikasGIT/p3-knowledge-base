# How to upgrade your plan

From **Settings → Plan**, or from an upgrade prompt you encounter elsewhere in the app (like hitting a storage or gallery limit), select the plan you want and continue to checkout.

## What happens during checkout

You'll see a summary of the plan, its price, and billing period (monthly or annual), then a secure payment form powered by Razorpay, P3's payment processor. Complete the payment there.

## The brief wait afterward

After payment, you'll land on a short processing screen rather than an instant confirmation. This is intentional: P3 confirms your new plan is active only once it receives confirmation directly from Razorpay, rather than trusting the checkout screen alone — a small extra step that protects against billing errors. This typically resolves within a few seconds.

If it takes longer than expected, you'll see a message letting you know your plan will activate once payment is confirmed, and that you can check back shortly. Your payment isn't lost or in question during this — it's just the confirmation catching up.

## After upgrading

Your new limits and features apply immediately once your plan shows as active. There's no need to do anything else — existing galleries and content are unaffected by an upgrade.
