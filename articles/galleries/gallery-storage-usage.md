# How is gallery storage calculated?

Storage usage is measured by the actual size of your photos as stored on P3's servers — which is the *encrypted* size, not necessarily identical to your original file size. Encryption typically adds a small, fixed amount of overhead per file, so your storage usage may be marginally higher than the sum of your original files, though the difference is usually negligible.

This is measured server-side from the actual data received, rather than trusted from what your device reports before upload — a deliberate accuracy and integrity choice, not something you need to think about day to day.

## Where to see your usage

Your current usage against your plan's limit is shown on your dashboard and in **Settings → Plan**, as a storage gauge. It updates as you upload and delete photos.

## What counts toward your limit

Every photo across every gallery you own counts toward your storage limit. Photos in galleries you've been invited to as a collaborator don't count against *your* storage — they count against the gallery owner's.
