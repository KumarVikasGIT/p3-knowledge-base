# Deleting a gallery

From **Gallery → Settings**, scroll to the danger zone at the bottom and select **Delete gallery**.

You'll be asked to type the gallery's exact name to confirm — this is a deliberate extra step to prevent accidental deletion, since this action cannot be undone.

## What happens when you delete a gallery

- All photos in the gallery are permanently removed, along with their encrypted data.
- Every collaborator and client with access to the gallery loses that access immediately.
- The storage used by the gallery's photos is reclaimed and reflected in your account's storage usage.

Deletion is a soft-delete initially, with permanent cleanup happening shortly after — but from your perspective and your collaborators', it's effectively immediate and irreversible. There's no recovery option once you've confirmed.

## Before you delete

If you only want to stop new people from accessing a gallery — not destroy it entirely — consider [Removing someone from a gallery](./3-7-remove-collaborator.md) or [Revoking or regenerating a share link](./3-6-share-link-revoke.md) instead. Deletion is for when you genuinely want the gallery and everything in it gone for good.
