# What can I do on the free plan?

The Free plan gets you a real, working account — not a crippled trial. It includes:

- A baseline storage allowance
- A limited number of galleries
- A limited number of clients (Professional accounts)

There's no time limit and no feature countdown. Free is a genuine tier, not a trial period.

## What it doesn't include

The higher storage and gallery ceilings of Personal and Pro, and some professional-tier features may be plan-gated.

For exact current numbers and pricing, see [What are the P3 plans and what's included?](../billing/plan-overview.md) — that's the single source of truth for limits, kept current as pricing or limits change.

## No hidden catch

P3 doesn't show ads or sell data on the free tier the way some "free" cloud storage products do. The platform is end-to-end encrypted by design, which means P3 can't read your photo content even if it wanted to — so there's nothing to mine or monetize regardless of plan.
