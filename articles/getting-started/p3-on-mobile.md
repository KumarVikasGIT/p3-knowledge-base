## Web app (PWA)

P3's web app can be installed to your home screen on both iOS and Android, giving you an app-like experience without needing an app store.

**On iOS (Safari):**
1. Open p3.app in Safari
2. Tap the Share icon
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open p3.app in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (⋮)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen** or **Install app**

The web app supports the full P3 experience, including end-to-end encrypted upload and gallery viewing — it runs the same crypto in your browser as the desktop site.
