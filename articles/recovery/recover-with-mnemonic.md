# Recovering with your recovery phrase

If you're logging in on a new device, or your browser data was cleared, P3 will guide you to a recovery screen automatically once it detects your key isn't present locally.

## Steps

1. Enter your 12-word recovery phrase, one word per box. If you copied it somewhere, pasting all 12 words at once into the first box will distribute them automatically.
2. P3 checks the phrase is well-formed before doing anything else — this catches typos or words from outside the standard wordlist immediately, before any other processing.
3. Select **Restore access**. Your device regenerates your decryption key from the phrase and compares it against what's on record for your account.
4. If it matches, your key is restored on this device and you're taken to your dashboard. Your galleries will be available, including ones shared with you by others.

## If the phrase doesn't match

You'll see a clear message that it doesn't match your account, with nothing changed — P3 deliberately does not overwrite or alter anything on a failed attempt, so there's no risk in trying again carefully. Double-check each word for typos, and confirm you're using the phrase for the correct account if you have more than one.

## What happens after a successful recovery

You'll see a short progress screen while P3 loads your galleries and decrypts their keys for this device — for accounts with a lot of content, this can take a few moments. This is normal and only needs to happen once per device.
