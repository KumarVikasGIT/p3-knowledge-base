# Logging in on a new device

Logging into your P3 account on a new device works in two parts: signing in, and then restoring your decryption key.

## Why your password alone isn't enough

Your account password gets you logged in, but it doesn't restore your photo decryption key — that key lives only on devices you've specifically set it up on, never on P3's servers. A new device, by definition, doesn't have it yet. This is expected, not an error.

## What happens

After logging in with your email and password, P3 will detect that this device doesn't have your key yet and guide you to a recovery step. You'll need either:

- Your recovery phrase, or
- Your backup password, if you set one up

See [What are my options if I lose access?](./recovery-options-overview.md) for the full picture of these two paths.

## After recovery

Once your key is restored on the new device, P3 loads your galleries and sets up decryption locally — you'll see a brief progress screen while this happens, especially if you have a lot of content. After that, this device works just like any other device you've used before, and you won't need to repeat this process unless you clear your browser data or switch devices again.
